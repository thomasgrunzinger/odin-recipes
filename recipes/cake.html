Piece of Cake

Description

Starting off with something that shouldn't be too difficult. Actually, not even remotely close. Like really this is so easy it should be a...

What You'll Need

  • 1 Cake
  • An object that can be used to seperate a section from it's whole
  • The immense fortitude and wherewithal to complete this monumental task.

Directions

  1. Place Cake in front of you.
  2. Lift your object in a manner that is conducive to completing a movement in a 90 degree motion.
  3. Steady.
  4. Lower your object till it meets the cake.
  5. Press down into the cake.
  6. Remove object from cake.
  7. Rotate the object as much as you would like, above cake.
  8. Press down into the cake again.
  9. Remove a section of the cake.
  10. Phew.
